RACCOONS

Welcome to Raccoons!

Defend your grill from the hotdog snatching garbage boys.

How to Play:

  • Click a hot dog to select.
  • Click the grill where you want to place the hot dog.
  • Defend your territory!
  • If you'd like to restart at any time, click the "Reset" button at the bottom of the page.

You win!

Those pesky raccoons don't know what hit 'em. You really are the grill master!

You lose.

They got your dogs. Guess those grubby paws are good for something.
