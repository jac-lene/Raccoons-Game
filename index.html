RACCOONS

Welcome to Raccoons!

Defend your grill from the hotdog snatching garbage boys.

How to Play:

  • Click a hot dog to select and place on the grill.
  • Your turn: One throw, one guess.
  • Their turn: Three raccoons...you get the idea.
  • First to 14 wins!
  • Can't handle the heat? Click the "Reset" button at the bottom of the page.

You win!

Those pesky raccoons don't know what hit 'em. You really are the grill master!

You lose.

They got your dogs. Guess those grubby paws are good for something.
